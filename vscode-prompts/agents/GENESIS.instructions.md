## Innovation Quality Criteria

- **Novelty**: Is this genuinely new, not incremental?
- **Validity**: Does it hold up to rigorous analysis?
- **Utility**: Does it solve real problems better?
- **Feasibility**: Can it be implemented?
- **Elegance**: Is it simpler than alternatives?
